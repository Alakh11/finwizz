LOAN

AGAINST PROPERTY

LAP loan can be availed by salaried professionals, self-employed individuals and also those who have a stable source of income such as rental income. A minimum income criterion applies to ensure repayment capacity.

A Loan Against Property (LAP) allows borrowers to leverage the value of their residential or commercial property and obtain funds for business expansion, debt consolidation, education, medical emergencies, or other personal and professional needs.

Features of Loan Against Property:

The loan is secured in nature.

Available only against clear and marketable property.

Long repayment tenure ranging from 7 to 15 years.

Lower interest rates compared to unsecured loans.

Funds can be used for personal or business purposes.

Ideal for debt consolidation and business expansion.

Higher loan eligibility based on property value.

Can be structured as overdraft or dropline overdraft.

Documents required:

Income Proof

Income Tax Returns with Computation

FAQS ON LOAN AGAINST PROPERTY

WHO CAN AVAIL LAP? +

Salaried professionals, self-employed individuals and people with stable income sources such as rental income can avail LAP.

WHAT IS THE DIFFERENCE BETWEEN A HOME LOAN AND LAP? +

Home loans are meant for buying or constructing a house, while LAP allows borrowing against an already owned property for multiple purposes.

CAN I USE LAP FOR ANY PERSONAL PURPOSE? +

Yes. LAP can be used for personal, professional or business requirements.

CAN I CLOSE OTHER LOANS WITH LAP? +

Yes. Many borrowers use LAP to consolidate higher-cost loans into a lower-interest secured facility.

HOW MUCH LOAN CAN I GET? +

Typically 50%–70% of the market value depending upon property type, borrower profile and lender policies.

CAN I GET AN OVERDRAFT AGAINST MY PROPERTY? +

Yes. Many lenders offer overdraft facilities where interest is charged only on the utilized amount.

CAN I GET LAP ON A LEASED PROPERTY? +

Yes. Lease Rental Discounting (LRD) is a popular product where rental income is considered while evaluating eligibility.

CAN I GET TAX BENEFITS ON LAP? +

Generally no direct tax benefits are available as in home loans, though interest may be claimed as a business expense where applicable.

CAN I SELL THE PROPERTY DURING THE LOAN TENURE? +

Yes, subject to lender approval and closure or transfer of outstanding dues.

CAN I GET LAP ON A PLOT? +

Most private banks do not fund vacant plots, though some nationalized banks may consider them.

CAN I GET A LOAN AGAINST AN INDUSTRIAL SHED? +

Some lenders may consider industrial properties based on structure, valuation and lending policies.
