LOAN

FOR DOCTORS

The life savers deserve the best! Loan for Doctors ensures that along with appreciation for the work they diligently do every single day, we strive to give them confidence to put their best foot forward in the toughest humanitarian job. After all, it is a privilege to save lives but heart-wrenching when you lose one.

And therefore, to commemorate these fighters, unsecured loans for Doctors have no end-use restrictions, require no collateral, and come with longer tenures, making it the perfect solution to meet financial goals stress-free.

If you are a doctor looking for a Personal Loan, we have a range of easy loans designed and catered especially for individual doctors & medical practitioners at the best rates with quick approvals and disbursements.

Loan for doctors can be availed for:

Any personal purpose including objectives for meeting expenses of professional requirement.

Development of clinic / clinic-cum-residence, nursing home, pathological laboratory.

Purchase of medical / diagnostic equipment, setting up of operation theatre, etc.

Purchase of office equipment viz. computers, air-conditioners and furniture, etc.

Expansion / renovation / modernization of existing premises / clinic / nursing home.

Working capital requirement including stock of medicines.
