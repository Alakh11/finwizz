The Finwizz Process – How It Works

Apply -
At this stage, you will need to fill in your details on the ‘Apply Now’ page and we will get in touch with you at the earliest.
Eligibility Check +
Our experts will analyze your loan requirement over the phone. We will need the below information to recommend the most suitable loan product with the lowest interest rate:

a. Mode of employment, years of work experience, Company you work for.
b. Your current income.
c. City of Employment & Current obligations (Any existing EMI’s being paid, credit card outstanding).
Document Collection +
The photocopies of the following documents will be picked up at your doorstep by our team.

a. KYC documents (ID & Residence proof)
b. Income documents
Loan Processing +
We will help you process the loan application with the bank and comply with bank formalities.
Loan Approval +
The bank will approve your loan application post submission and verification of your documents.
Disbursement +
Amount will be credited to your bank account after submission of post-sanction documents.
