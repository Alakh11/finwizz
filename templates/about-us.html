The dynamic journey of Finwizz is hinged on one simple philosophy that everyone deserves to live their lives to their fullest potential.

The seed that was sown way back in 2005 by three passionate individuals from the banking sector has reaped numerous milestones as we walk through the journey of making transformative impact in the lives of people. Yet, the most valuable asset we’ve reaped is the unwavering faith of our customers borne from our credibility to help them make responsible decisions about their finance.

Over the years our services have enhanced, we have evolved and our business has grown. But what has remained as solid as a rock is our core philosophy of making loans accessible to people from all walks of life. 1000 fulfilled dreams across the country and crores of loans later, we give ourselves a pat on the back knowing someone, somewhere is living a life of their dreams with the support of FINWIZZ.

The journey that begun with three individuals is today a culmination of a dynamic workforce contributing to the growth of the company. At Finwizz, we strive to create an equal opportunity workplace where hard-work is rewarded and talent nurtured.

Syed Haseeb

Founder / Director

Syed Haseeb had been an adept manager of Personal Loans in ICICI Bank before he was bitten by the entrepreneurship bug. With over 20 years of experience in Banking, this tech maverick is driven by solving problems and developing long-term relationships with customers. His authenticity is evident in every interaction he has at Finwizz and beyond. He is based in Mumbai and loves to indulge in water sports with family.

Anil Mathew

Founder / Director

Known as an compliance marvel of sorts within the team, Anil Mathew holds an MBA in Finance and brings 23 years of experience in Banking & Capital Markets. He started his career with Cargill Investor Services and later joined ICICI Bank in its unsecured business division. He was with Citibank NA in its Mortgage Business just before the inception of Finwizz. A natural with numbers, he is energised by compliance challenges. Based in Pune, he loves exploring new places with his family.

Rajeev Singhvi

Founder / Director

Armed with double degrees in Law & Commerce, our Legal Wizard Rajeev Singhvi has over 25 years of experience in Banking & Finance. He has been associated with Escorts Finance, Apple Finance & Transamerica Finance. His last endeavour was with ICICI Bank as Regional Manager of Personal Loans, in Mumbai. A perfectionist by nature, unwavering in outlook and a thoughtful mentor to the Finwizz team, he leads by example and makes sure the growth goes beyond and above business. He is based in Ahmedabad, an innate sports enthusiast, where you are most likely to run into him on tennis courts or in the wilderness off-roading along with his friends and family.
